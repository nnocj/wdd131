The reason for naming my site AbodeConnect is find a catchy, self-explanatory brand name that expresses the mission to help housing property seekers connect to owners.

Purpose

The purpose of this app is to help people successfully find residential apartments especially in cities.

Audience

Are the properties verified?
Yes they are verified by our team. Property owners are required to submit details of their properties to us after which our verification review submitted document, contact owners, and carry out an inspection on the Property before listing it on the app.

Do I make direct payment to property owners?
No, We serve as intermediary between property owners and tenants, so payments are made to us then we pay the tenants.
